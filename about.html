Who developed the web application?

The application was made by Owen Brouwer, a CMGT student at Hogeschool Rotterdam. He was bron on November 7th in 2002.


For what reason did they make this application?

The application was made with one main purpose in mind, to encourage people to schedule a checkup with their doctors. There are more than 38 thousand deaths caused by heart disease in the netherlands alone. Among these deaths are a lot of people that didnt even know they had a heart disease. For this single reason we are trying to give people an insight into their health.


The AI said i have a heart disease, am i going to die?

The AI compares your given data with existing data to determin if you have heart disease. The AI does this with an accuracy of 87%. There is no reason to panic if the AI says you have a heart disease, since there is always a chance that the AI is wrong. That is why we implore you to make a appointment with your doctor, so you can check if you really do have heart disease. This way you will most likely be able to get it treated right away.


Why would i go to the doctor when the AI tells me i have no heart disease?

The AI compares you given data with existing data to determin if you have heart disease. The AI does this with an accuracy of 87%. When the AI says to you that you don't have heart disease there is always a possibility it could be wrong. Thats why we implore you to make a appointment with your doctor to get it checked, just to be sure.
